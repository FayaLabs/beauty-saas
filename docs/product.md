# Glow Studio — Product Overview

## The Niche
Beauty & wellness businesses: hair salons, nail studios, spas, barbershops, aesthetics clinics, and independent beauty professionals.

## Target Audience
- Salon owners managing 1-20 staff
- Independent stylists and beauty professionals
- Spa and wellness center managers
- Beauty franchise operators

## Core Pain Points
1. **Scheduling chaos** — Appointments managed via WhatsApp, paper, or spreadsheets. Double bookings, no-shows, and last-minute cancellations cost revenue daily.
2. **No client history** — Professionals can't remember what formula they used last time, what the client's preferences are, or their treatment history.
3. **Cash register blindness** — End-of-day cash doesn't match. No visibility into which services generate the most revenue or which professionals are most productive.
4. **Commission headaches** — Calculating staff commissions manually each month is error-prone and causes disputes.
5. **Client retention** — No system to follow up after visits, send birthday messages, or remind clients to return.

## Key Features
- **Agenda** — Calendar with drag-and-drop, multi-professional view, and WhatsApp confirmations
- **Client Database** — Full profile with photos, service history (before/after), notes, and preferences
- **Beauty Journey** — Visual timeline of treatments per client with before/after photos
- **Financial** — Invoicing, cash register, receivables/payables, credit card tracking
- **Inventory** — Product stock with automatic deduction per service execution
- **Marketing** — Automated messages for birthdays, return reminders, and post-service follow-ups
- **Staff Management** — Schedules, permissions, and commission rules
- **Online Booking** — Public booking page with service/professional selection
- **Reports** — Revenue, occupancy, no-show rates, professional performance

## Differentiator
The **Beauty Journey** — a visual treatment timeline per client that no generic scheduling tool offers. Professionals can see the full history of colors, treatments, and results at a glance.

## Pricing Positioning
- **Starter** ($29/mo) — Solo professionals, basic scheduling + clients
- **Professional** ($79/mo) — Growing salons, full feature set
- **Enterprise** ($199/mo) — Multi-location, custom branding, API

## Key Metrics to Track
- Monthly active salons
- Appointments booked per month
- Client retention rate (return within 60 days)
- Average revenue per salon
